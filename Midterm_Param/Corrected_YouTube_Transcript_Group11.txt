Voi nhom mo hinh CNN-LSTM/GRU, nguyen ly chung la dua chuoi dac trung tieng noi
vao CNN truoc. CNN co nhiem vu hoc cac pattern cuc bo trong tin hieu, vi du
nhu su tang giam nang luong, su thay doi MFCC, hoac cac vung pho co dac trung
noi bat. Sau do, LSTM hoac GRU tiep tuc xu ly chuoi feature map theo thoi gian.
LSTM co cac cong input, forget va output gate de quyet dinh nen giu lai thong
tin nao va bo qua thong tin nao. GRU gon hon LSTM, thuong co update gate va
reset gate, nen nhe hon ve tinh toan.

Voi nhom attention/co-attention, mo hinh hoc trong so cho cac frame hoac cac
nguon thong tin khac nhau. Trong bai toan SER, khong phai moi thoi diem trong
audio deu the hien cam xuc ro nhu nhau. Vi vay attention pooling giup mo hinh
tap trung vao cac frame co cue cam xuc manh hon. Co-attention mo rong y tuong
nay bang cach cho hai hoac nhieu representation tuong tac voi nhau. Vi du,
embedding emotion2vec co the lam query de hoi temporal branch va spectrogram
branch xem cue acoustic nao phu hop voi thong tin cam xuc da hoc truoc.

Voi huong pretrained representation, waveform duoc dua vao mot model da duoc
pretrain tren speech/emotion data. Model nay xuat ra embedding co kich thuoc co
dinh. Sau do embedding duoc dua qua MLP adapter hoac classifier nho de phan
loai cam xuc. Uu diem la tan dung duoc tri thuc hoc truoc, giam ap luc phai
train mot mo hinh lon tu dau tren bo du lieu sinh vien co tai nguyen han che.

Voi huong feature engineering, audio duoc trich xuat thanh cac dac trung thu
cong. MFCC mo ta timbre hay spectral envelope, RMS mo ta cuong do/nang luong,
ZCR mo ta do sac va tinh voiced/unvoiced, con cac spectral features mo ta do
sang, do rong va phan bo nang luong theo tan so. Cac dac trung nay co the duoc
dua vao SVM/RF/Logistic Regression hoac dung lam mot branch phu trong mo hinh
multi-representation.

Giai phap de xuat cua nhom la mo hinh 06D Emotion2Vec Co-Attention SER. Day la

dien khac nhau. Ly do chon huong nay la cam xuc trong giong noi khong nam tron
trong mot dac trung duy nhat. No co the nam trong timbre, nang luong, su thay
doi theo thoi gian, cau truc pho, hoac cac cue cam xuc muc cao ma pretrained
model hoc duoc.

Kien truc 06D gom bon branch. Branch A la temporal acoustic branch, nhan input
la chuoi MFCC, delta MFCC, delta-delta MFCC, RMS, ZCR va cac spectral features.
Branch nay dung 1D-CNN de hoc pattern cuc bo, BiLSTM de hoc ngu canh hai chieu
theo thoi gian, va attention pooling de chon cac frame quan trong.

Branch B la spectrogram branch, nhan input la log-Mel spectrogram, delta
log-Mel va delta-delta log-Mel. Day la bieu dien time-frequency, nen phu hop
voi 2D-CNN. Trong 06D, branch nay dung residual CNN block va SE/channel
attention de nhan manh cac feature map co cue cam xuc ro.

Branch C la emotion pretrained branch. Waveform 16 kHz duoc dua vao frozen
emotion2vec de lay emotion embedding. Sau do embedding nay duoc dua qua adapter
MLP de dua ve khong gian embedding chung. Nhom giu emotion2vec o trang thai
frozen de tiet kiem GPU va giam rui ro overfit.

Branch D la statistical branch. Tu cac acoustic features, nhom tinh cac thong
ke nhu mean, standard deviation, min, max, median va IQR de tao vector thong ke
co dinh. Vector nay duoc dua vao MLP va RBF-SVM side model. Muc dich cua branch
nay la bo sung global descriptors va giup mo hinh co them mot goc nhin classical
feature engineering.

Sau khi co embedding tu cac branch, mo hinh dung Emotion-Guided Co-Attention.
Trong do, z_e2v tu emotion2vec lam query, con z_temporal va z_spectral lam
key/value. Co the hieu truc quan la embedding emotion2vec hoi hai branch
acoustic xem cue nao phu hop voi cam xuc dang xet. Sau do, cac vector duoc
concat va dua qua Fusion MLP, classifier va stacking ensemble de tao xac suat
cuoi cung cho 6 emotion.
